**Welcome to Design Signals Quick Bake**

If you're like me, running a business means economic stability isn't just a talking point — it's the difference between a good quarter and a scramble.

Supply chain gets disrupted by tariff policy. International trade agreements shift. Resources get repriced overnight. Three weeks ago I found myself deep in a rabbit hole — building small intelligence tools with AI to help make sense of it all. Supply chain planning. Apparel design pipelines. The kind of signals that used to require a whole analyst team, now surfaced in a dashboard I built on a Tuesday.

That's what this newsletter is.

**Design Signals Quick Bake** is a short dispatch from Green Neurons — my Fort Worth-based consultancy at the intersection of technical apparel development and wearable technology. Each issue covers two things:

— **Color intelligence** from the runway, distilled into palette groups you can actually use in production decisions  
— **Supply chain signals** from global shipping lanes, tariff desks, and trade corridors that affect where and how you source

No fluff. No trend mood boards. Just the signals worth acting on.

**Follow along if you are:**
- Planning fashion merchandise for the next season
- Taking an innovative apparel or wearable tech concept to production
- Sourcing internationally and watching tariff and transit risk
- Just genuinely curious about how the apparel supply chain actually works

The tools that power this newsletter live at **[greenneurons.us](https://greenneurons.us)** — free to explore, no login required.

Peace out. 🤙

— Jenny Siede  
Green Neurons Design + Development  
Fort Worth, TX
