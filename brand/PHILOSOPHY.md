# Green Neurons — Brand Philosophy
*Last updated: May 2026*

---

## Who We Are

Green Neurons Design and Technologies PBC is a small, deliberate company built at the intersection of intelligent design systems and emerging technology. We are based in Fort Worth, Texas. We are a public benefit corporation, which means our work is accountable to something larger than revenue.

We are not a consultancy that scales by headcount. We are not an agency with a pitch deck for every occasion. We are a founder-led practice that takes on work we believe in, with people worth knowing.

---

## What We Believe

**Reputation is the product.**
Everything else — deliverables, decks, demos — is evidence of it. We build slowly and protect fiercely.

**Earn the detail.**
Lead with humanity. Follow with capability. No fluff until there is a reason for it. The right time to explain what we do in full is after someone has decided they want to listen.

**Genuine over transactional.**
We write letters when we could send emails. We make introductions when we could stay quiet. The world can always use a genuine handshake.

**Small is not an apology.**
Deliberate, focused, and relationship-driven is a strategy — not a limitation we are working to overcome.

**Human tone is non-negotiable.**
Not warm-corporate. Not AI-smooth. Actually human. If a sentence sounds like it was generated, it gets rewritten until it does not.

**The problem before the solution.**
We do not arrive with answers. We arrive with the right questions and the patience to follow them somewhere useful.

## Brand Voice

Green Neurons communicates like a person who has done the work and has nothing to prove.

| Do | Do Not |
|---|---|
| Direct and grounded | Jargon-forward |
| Confident without overselling | Overpromising |
| Warm but not effusive | Corporate fluff |
| Specific and concrete | Vague value statements |
| Human and considered | AI-bot smooth |
| TL;DR discipline | Verbose for the sake of it |

**Tone by context:**

- *Cold introduction:* Warm, brief, curious. Open a door, do not walk through it uninvited.
- *Proposal or pitch:* Precise. Every word earns its place. No filler paragraphs.
- *Client communication:* Collegial, direct, accountable. Say what you will do. Do it.
- *Public writing (Substack, web):* Voice-forward. Opinion welcome. Fluff unwelcome.
